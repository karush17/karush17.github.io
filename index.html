Karush Suri

Hi! I am a research engineer at Recursion with Dominique Beaini. Previously I was a research engineer at Google X with Rishabh Singh and Lam Nguyen, and a student researcher at Borealis AI. I completed my M.A.Sc in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto with Yuri Lawryshyn and Kostas Plataniotis. I earned my B.Tech in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Amity University where I was affiliated as an undergraduate research assistant with Rinki Gupta. Besides research I enjoy swimming and reading comic books.

[Github]   [Google Scholar]   [LinkedIn]   [Twitter]   [Email]

I aim to create sequential agents which possess a higher degree of generality and flexibility. These agents must learn representations that stabilize pretraining and accelerate downstream transfer. Towards this goal, I develop algorithms in Reinforcement Learning and Unsupervised Learning by leveraging advances from foundational models.
